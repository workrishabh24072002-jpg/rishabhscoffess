Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Rishabh's Coffee — our products, process, and delivery.

Home/FAQ
We grind your beans to your chosen specification right before packing, locking in freshness and aroma. Select Whole Bean if you have a grinder at home (best flavour). Choose Espresso Grind for espresso machines and moka pot. Filter Grind suits pour over, AeroPress, and drip. Cafetière Grind is coarser, ideal for French press.
Very fresh. We roast every Tuesday and dispatch within 24 hours of roasting. Most customers in Ireland receive their order within 1–3 days of it being roasted — far fresher than any supermarket or high-street coffee brand.
Standard tracked delivery across Ireland takes 2–4 working days from dispatch. Orders over €50 receive free tracked delivery. Express options are available at checkout.
Store in an airtight container at room temperature, away from direct sunlight and moisture. Do not refrigerate — temperature fluctuations cause condensation which degrades flavour. Best consumed within 4 weeks of roast date for whole bean, 2–3 weeks for pre-ground.
We accept returns on unopened bags within 30 days of purchase. As coffee is a consumable product, we cannot accept returns on opened bags unless there is a quality issue. Contact hello@rishabhscoffee.com with your order number and we'll resolve it immediately.

We supply cafés, restaurants, and offices across Ireland. Email hello@rishabhscoffee.com with your business name, location, and estimated weekly volume. Trade pricing starts from 5kg per order.
We source directly from smallholder farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, paying above fair-trade prices. We visit every farm we source from at least once per harvest season. Our Eco-Line uses BASF Bioflex with up to 60% less waste than conventional manufacturing.
All our coffees are naturally vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free. Flavoured coffees (French Vanilla, Hazelnut Brew, etc.) contain natural flavouring compounds added during roasting. They do not contain allergens but are processed in a facility that handles nuts — contact us if you have a severe allergy.
