Our Story

Born from a
Love of Coffee.

Since 1975

A Family Passion,
Five Decades Strong

Rishabh's Coffee began in 1975 when our founder, driven by an obsession with the perfect cup, started sourcing single-origin beans directly from smallholder farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. What started as a passion project has grown into a thriving specialty roastery — but the principles have never changed.

We believe great coffee starts at the farm. That's why we pay above fair-trade prices, visit every farm we source from, and invest in the communities that grow our beans. Every bag you buy is a vote for a more sustainable, equitable coffee industry.

Today we roast in small batches every week — never more than we need, never less than perfect. We ship within 24 hours of roasting, because you deserve the freshest coffee possible.

Our Values

🌱

Sustainability First

Every decision considers its impact on the planet. From eco-packaging to carbon-offset shipping, we're committed to doing better — not just talking about it.

🤝

Direct Relationships

We work directly with farmers, cutting out middlemen and paying above fair-trade prices. We visit every farm because we believe in looking people in the eye.

⚗️

Uncompromising Quality

Every roast is tasted by our Q-grader. Every bag inspected before dispatch. We send nothing out that we wouldn't proudly serve to our own family.
